Our Roots in Holistic Healing

Founded with a vision to bring peace, healing, and holistic health into people's lives, our journey is deeply rooted in personal transformation and family inspiration.

The Power of Personal Healing

Our story begins with Shri Prakash Pawar, the founder's father, who faced two severe accidents that became the foundation of our therapeutic approach.

  • ►

    Spinal Recovery: Years ago, a severe spinal injury from a work accident led doctors to recommend yoga. Through dedication and faith, he healed himself completely with consistent yogic practice.

  • ►

    Second Chance: Later, a serious accident when a coconut tree fell on his head once again reaffirmed yoga as his path to full and complete recovery.

★

Witnessing Transformation

Around the same period, the founder's mother suffered a paralysis attack. Under Shri Prakash Pawar care, leveraging yogic practices and natural therapies, she gradually recovered and regained her strength. Witnessing both parents heal through the power of yoga became the greatest inspiration, solidifying a life-long commitment to holistic wellness.

Our Timeline of Service

2018

The First Step

Founded Swarg Yog Chikitsa Kendra in Morgaon, following a realization of a true purpose: dedicating life to serving people through yoga and holistic healing.

2022

Establishing in Pune

The dream expanded with the establishment of Pawar's Yog Therapy Center at Manjari Budruk, Pune, quickly becoming a trusted space for transformation and wellness.

Today

Family Mission

Inspired by the journey, the founder's three sisters joined the mission, working together to spread health, happiness, and peace through Yoga and natural therapies.
